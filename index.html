The Future of Meal Prep & Leftovers

Where premium glass containers meet the Jetsons.

Crystal-clear, durable, eco-friendly — and definitely not your grandma's Tupperware.

A kitchen-mounted hub with wall or magnetic attachment scans generic glass container-compatible silicone lids via camera or NFC to support two pathways: (1) leftover tracking for waste reduction and (2) proactive meal-prep batch management. Lids feature embedded LED lights (green fresh/week-ready, yellow pulse eat-soon/mid-week, red urgent/tonight’s star) synced via BLE from the hub. A compact digital scale auto-tares weight on placement; voice input updates usage or additions (e.g., “used 2 oz roast chicken” or “added 4 oz grilled chicken”). The system tracks freshness timelines, macros/calories from cloud APIs, and generates simple recipes prioritizing near-expiry items in either pathway. No invasive food sensors — predictive via usage patterns and scale data. Delivers glowing visual cues for effortless leftover rescue or zero-waste meal-prep planning.
